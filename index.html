Ravioli Lasagna

  • Time: 1 Hour 25 Minutes
  • Serves: 8
Ingredients Quantity
Olive Oil 2 Tablaspoons
Diced Sweet Onion 1
Minced Garlic Cloves 2
Ground Beef 1 Pound
Salt 1 Teaspoon
Freshly Ground Pepper 3/4 Teaspoon
Dried Oregano 1 Teaspoon
Marinara Sauce 1 and 1/2 Cups
Thawed Ravioli 2 Pounds
Ricotta Cheese 1 and 1/2 Cups
Shredded Mozzarella Cheese 2 Cups
Chopped Fresh Basil 3 Tablespoons

Directions:

Step 1:Preheat the oven to 375°F. Lightly grease a 9-by-13-inch casserole dish with nonstick spray.

Step 2:In a large skillet, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the onion and sauté until translucent, 4 to 5 minutes. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, 1 minute more.

Step 3:Add the ground beef and sauté until fully cooked, 6 to 8 minutes. Season with the salt, pepper and oregano.

Step 4:Stir in the marinara sauce and bring to a simmer over medium-low heat. Simmer until the sauce thickens slightly and has good flavor, 20 minutes.

Step 5:While the sauce cooks, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. When it comes to a rolling boil, add the ravioli and cook according to the instructions on the package. Drain well.

Step 6:To build the lasagna, place a layer of ravioli in the base of the pan. Ladle a quarter of the sauce over the ravioli, then top with ½ cup ricotta cheese, spread into a layer. Sprinkle with a ¼ cup mozzarella.

Step 7:Repeat the layering process two more times. For the fourth and final layer, add one more layer of ravioli, sauce and mozzarella (no ricotta this time).

Step 8:Bake the lasagna until the sauce is bubbly and the cheese is melty, 25 to 30 minutes. Let cool at least 10 minutes before slicing and serving. Garnish with basil and serve warm.

Corn Fritter Caprese with Peaches and Tomatoes

  • Time: 1 Hour
  • Serves: 6
Ingredients Quantity
(Corn Fritters)
Scallions,Thinly Sliced 1 Bunch
Fresh Corn Kernels 3 Cups
Chopped Fresh Parsley 1/4 Cup
Chopped Fresh basil 2 Tablespoons
Kosher Salt 3/4 Tablespoon
Freshly Ground Black Pepper 1 Teaspoon
Crushed Red-Pepper Flakes 1/2 Teaspoon
All-Purpose Flour 1 Cup
Baking Powder 1 Teaspoon
Whisked Eggs 2 Large one's
Half-and-Half 1/4 Cup
Shredded Mozzarella Cheese 1 Cup
Grated Parmesan Cheese 1/3 Cup
Vegetable Oil 3 Tablespoons
(Assembly)
Thickly Sliced Tomatoes 3
Thickly Sliced Peaches 4
Thickly Sliced Fresh Mozzarella 12 Ounces
Roughly torn Basil leaves 1/2 Cup
Extra-Virgin Olive Oil As needed
Flaky Sea salt and freshly ground black Pepper As needed

Directions:

Step 1:In a large bowl, toss the scallions, corn, parsley, basil, salt, pepper and red-pepper flakes. Add the flour and baking powder, and toss until the mixture is well combined.

Step 2:Add the eggs and half-and-half and then mix with a spatula until a thick batter forms. Fold in the mozzarella and Parmesan.

Step 3:In a large skillet over medium heat, heat the vegetable oil. While it heats, line a baking sheet with paper towels.

Step 4:When the oil is hot, scoop ¼-cup portions of batter into the skillet. You’ll need to work in batches, cooking just a few at a time. Cook until golden brown, about 3 minutes, then flip and cook until golden on the other side, about 2 to 3 minutes more.

Step 5:When the fritters are cooked, transfer to the prepared baking sheet. Continue until you’ve used all the batter.

Step 6:To assemble, arrange the corn fritters, tomatoes, peaches and mozzarella slices on a large platter. Sprinkle basil evenly over the top. Garnish with a generous drizzle of olive oil, flaky salt and freshly ground black pepper.

Pasta Alla Norma with Eggplant, Basil & Pecorino

  • Time: 1 Hour
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
Extra-Virgin Olive Oil 4 Tablespoons
Eggplant Sliced into 1-Inch Strips 1 Large Eggplant
Kosher Salt and freshly ground Black Pepper As needed
Thinly Sliced Sweet Onion 1
Peeled and Crushed garlic Cloves 3
Can Of Crushed Tomatoes One 28-Ounce Can
Crushed Red Pepper Flakes 1 Teaspoon
Dried Oregano 3/4 Teaspoon
Bite-Sized dry Pasta, Like Rigatoni or Macaroni 1 Pound
Chopped fresh Parsley 1/4 Cup
Chopped Fresh Basil 1/4 Cup
Grated Pecorino or Ricotta Salata Cheese 1/2 Cup

Directions:

Step 1:In a large sauté pan, heat the olive oil over medium heat. Add the eggplant in batches and cook on all sides until golden brown. Remove the eggplant from the pan and set aside on a large plate. Season to taste with salt and pepper.

Step 2: To the same pan, add the onion and sauté until tender, about 4 minutes. Add the garlic and sauté until fragrant, about 1 minute more.

Step 3:Stir in the tomatoes and bring to a simmer. Add the red pepper flakes and oregano, and season with salt and pepper. Simmer until the flavor of the sauce develops and concentrates slightly, 15 to 20 minutes.

Step 4: While the sauce simmers, bring a large pot of salted water to a boil over high heat. Add the pasta and cook according to the instructions on the package. Drain well.

Step 5:Add the pasta and eggplant to the sauce; toss to coat. Add the parsley, basil and pecorino or ricotta salata, and toss well to combine.
